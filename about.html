The Sourdough Benefit

True sourdough is a living thing. Unlike store-bought bread that uses commercial yeast to rise in minutes, our dough undergoes a 24-hour slow fermentation. This process is transformative:

  • Gentle Digestion: The long rise breaks down much of the gluten and phytic acid, making it kinder to your gut.
  • Lower Glycemic Index: Sourdough's natural acids slow down the absorption of glucose, preventing the "sugar spikes" common in processed breads.
  • Bio-Available Nutrients: The fermentation unlocks vitamins and minerals in the grain that are otherwise inaccessible.
